Project 4 - MET Calendar (New Media)

This project presents highlighted artworks in the MET throughout the history. These artworks are usually the most famous artworks in the collection in different categories. The idea was generated from calendar souvenirs that are sold in lots of museums. I will create a digitalized calendar that pushes to users a new image and information of an artwork everyday.

Link: Exploring Clocks in MET

Process

I decided to use a mobile device because I hope to allow users to learn some knowledge but not heavy information from the MET collection everyday, and mobile device is the one users normally use everyday. The application includes three parts, single day, artwork information of a single day, and calendar.
